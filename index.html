Recent News

Bangladesh is undergoing a significant transformation known as "Bangladesh 2.0," following the ousting of former Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina in August 2024. This movement, led predominantly by students and supported by Nobel laureate Muhammad Yunus, aims to establish a more democratic, transparent, and inclusive society.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Bangladesh successfully launched its first geostationary communications satellite, Bangabandhu-1, on May 11, 2018, using a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket. Named after the country's founding leader, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, the satellite aims to enhance telecommunications, broadcasting, and internet services across the region.
